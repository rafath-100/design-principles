Design Principles in OOPS: A design is good iff if the cost of changing it is minimum, if it is lesss complex and if it is easy to test.

Following are the Design Principles:
1)DRY => Don't Repeat Yourself
2)KISS => Keep It Simple and Stupid
3)YAGNI => You Aren't Gonna Need It
4)SLAP => Single Level of Abstraction Principle
5)SOLID

SRP-Single Responsibility Principle
OCP-Open Closed Principle
LSP-Liskov Substitution Principle
ISP-Interface Segregation Principle
DIP-Dependency Inversion Principle
